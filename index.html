Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

HypeIntro ("we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile application that lets youth baseball teams manage walk-up songs, AI-generated announcer intros, and game day audio playback. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use HypeIntro, we may collect:

  • Account information — email address and authentication credentials when you sign in via Apple Sign In or email/password
  • Team and player data — team names, player names, jersey numbers, and walk-up song configurations that you create within the app
  • Music preferences — song selections, clip timing, and voice style choices used to configure walk-up intros
  • Usage data — app interactions, feature usage, and analytics events to help us improve the product

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the HypeIntro service
  • Generate AI announcer voice intros for your players
  • Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the app experience
  • Send service-related communications when necessary

Third-Party Services

HypeIntro uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Apple MusicKit — song search and playback via your Apple Music subscription. Apple Privacy Policy
  • ElevenLabs — AI voice generation for announcer intros, processed server-side. ElevenLabs Privacy Policy
  • Mixpanel — product analytics to understand feature usage. Mixpanel Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat / Apple StoreKit — subscription management and purchase processing

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Data is shared with third-party services only as described above and only to the extent necessary to provide the HypeIntro service.

Children's Privacy

HypeIntro is designed for use by parents and team organizers managing youth baseball teams. The app is operated by adults (parents, coaches, organizers) on behalf of their children's teams. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. Player names and jersey numbers are entered by the parent or organizer, not by the child. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information directly, please contact us at vibes@boringmarketing.com and we will promptly delete it.

Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen within the app. When you delete your account, we remove your profile, team data, player configurations, and associated audio files from our servers. Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for product improvement purposes.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security policies on our database, and secure server-side processing for voice generation. API keys for third-party services are stored server-side and are never exposed in the client application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at vibes@boringmarketing.com.
